Bill Split Manager

Bill Split Manager is an application that helps you manage all social expenses. With the Bill Split Manager, you no longer have to worry about owing your friend's expenses on concert tickets or travel hotel!

Split expenses

Find yourself struggling to split the bill with your friends after a night out? Well, worry no more! With Split Bill Manager, you can efficiently split bills and expenses with your friends without any hassle.

Group splitting feature

Create a group, add your friends, and enter the amount spent. The page then calculates the exact amount each person owes or is owed, making sure that everyone pays their fair share. You can even split the bill unevenly, if necessary, with different percentages for each person.
